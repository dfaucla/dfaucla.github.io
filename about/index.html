DFA@UCLA
We leverage design thinking for social and civic good in UCLA and the LA community.

Design for America aims to solve community problems through human-centered design. Every year, we recognize a problem in our area, formulate a project proposal and implement design-based solutions.

Join us if you are . . .

  • > interested in human-centered design or design innovation

  • > love solving real word problems through design thinking

  • > want more project experience in UI/UX

  • > want to join an interdisciplinary community of innovators
